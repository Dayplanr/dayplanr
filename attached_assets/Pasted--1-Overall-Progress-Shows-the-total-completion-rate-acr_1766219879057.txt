
1. Overall Progress
Shows the total completion rate across all active goals.
Helps users understand whether they are progressing in their long-term vision.
Example UI text: Overall Goal Progress Track how consistently you’re moving toward your long-term vision.
Metrics:
Total goals created
Goals in progress
Completed goals
Average completion percentage
✅ 2. Milestone Completion Rate
Every goal has milestones → this insight zeigt, wie gut Nutzer:innen ihre Milestones erledigen.
Example UI text: Milestone Completion Rate See how effectively you’re completing the steps that lead to your goals.
Metrics:
Completed milestones
Pending milestones
Best performing goal (based on milestone progress)
✅ 3. Category Breakdown
Damit Nutzer sehen, in welchem Lebensbereich sie ihre Ziele verfolgen.
Example categories:
Health
Work
Personal
Finance
Creativity
Education
Example UI text: Goal Categories Breakdown Understand where you invest most of your long-term energy.
Display:  Pie chart (keine Farben vorgegeben)
✅ 4. Challenge Completion Insight
Falls ein Goal ein “Challenge” hat (z. B. 30-day challenge):
Example UI text: Challenge Completion Track your progress through your active challenges.
Metrics:
Days completed
Days left
Streak for the challenge
Completion percentage
✅ 5. Goal Consistency
Messen, wie oft der User aktiv an einem Goal gearbeitet hat.
Example UI text: Consistency Score See how steadily you're progressing toward your goals.
Metrics:
Days with progress
Best streak
Last activity date
✅ 6. Most Motivating Goal (Based on Completion Speed)
Ein Insight, das zeigt, welches Goal am schnellsten vorangebracht wird.
Example UI text: Fastest-Progressing Goal The goal you’re moving toward the quickest.
✅ 7. Goals at Risk (little or zero progress)
Damit Nutzer erinnert werden: “Hey, hier passiert gerade nichts.”
Example UI text: Goals at Risk Goals that haven’t seen progress recently.
Metrics:
No progress in the last 7 days
Low completion (<10%)
✅ 8. Productivity Score for Goals
Weil du für Weekly/Monthly/Yearly bei Tasks auch einen Score hast → hier auch.
Example UI text: Goal Productivity Score A combined score based on your consistency, milestone completion, and progress.
Format:
If no progress → 0 (wie du wolltest)
Else dynamic score 1–100
